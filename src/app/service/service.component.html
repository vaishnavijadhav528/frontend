General Ward
A general ward in a hospital is a shared patient area designed to provide essential medical care in a communal setting.

Key features typically include:

Shared Accommodations: Multiple patients occupy a single room or an open-plan area, each assigned a bed with surrounding space.
Basic Amenities: Each patient area usually includes a bedside table, chair, and access to shared facilities such as bathrooms and showers.
Centralized Nursing Stations: Nurses' stations are strategically located within the ward to monitor patients effectively and respond promptly to their needs.
Cost-Effectiveness: General wards offer a more affordable option for inpatient care compared to private or semi-private rooms.
